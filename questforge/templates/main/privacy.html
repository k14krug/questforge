Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

When you use QuestForge, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, and password (securely hashed).
  • Game Data: Information related to your games, such as game states, campaign details, and player actions.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the platform, such as pages visited and actions performed.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the QuestForge platform.
  • Maintain persistent game states and campaign data.
  • Enhance your experience with AI-generated content.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Secure password storage using bcrypt hashing.
  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit using HTTPS.
  • Regular updates to our software to address security vulnerabilities.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of QuestForge, its users, or others.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about your privacy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@questforge.com
